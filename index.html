Who We Are

Engineered for the threats of tomorrow

Vigil Perception builds autonomous perception systems for defence and security. We fuse multiple sensing modalities into unified platforms that detect, classify, and track without human bottlenecks.

Our systems are purpose-built for contested environments: delivering persistent situational awareness across the full threat timeline.

We work to open standards and interoperable architectures, ensuring our technology integrates seamlessly into existing command and control infrastructure.

Our Approach

Intelligence at machine speed

01

Autonomous Decision-Making

Algorithmic orchestration that dynamically manages sensors based on the evolving threat picture. No manual cueing. No operator overload. The system decides what to look at, when, and why.

02

Multi-Source Fusion

Complementary data streams merged into a single, unified threat picture. Each sensing modality contributes its unique strengths, producing confidence levels that no single sensor could achieve alone.

03

False Alarm Rejection

Cross-validated measurements that autonomously filter out clutter, wildlife, and non-threats. If it isn't a genuine threat, the system will not waste your operators' time alerting on it.
